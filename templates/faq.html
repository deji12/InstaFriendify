FAQ

To add an Instagram account:
  1. Click on the Add Account button in the navbar.
  2. Enter your Instagram username, password, and the maximum number of followers you want to add to close friends.
  3. Click Submit to connect your account.
Note: Make sure your credentials are correct, as the bot will verify them before adding the account.

To get followers:
  1. Go to the Accounts page.
  2. Find the account you want to get followers for.
  3. Click the Get Followers button.
  4. The bot will start scraping followers and save them to your account.
Note: You can only get followers once per account unless you reset the followers list.

To add followers to Close Friends:
  1. Ensure you have already gotten followers for the account.
  2. Go to the Accounts page.
  3. Find the account you want to add followers to Close Friends for.
  4. Click the Add to Close Friends button.
  5. The bot will start adding followers to your Close Friends list.
Note: This process may take some time depending on the number of followers.

To reset followers for an account:
  1. Go to the Accounts page.
  2. Find the account you want to reset followers for.
  3. Click the Reset Followers button.
  4. This will clear the existing followers list, allowing you to scrape followers again.
Note: Resetting followers will delete the current list, so use this option carefully.

To delete an Instagram account:
  1. Go to the Accounts page.
  2. Find the account you want to delete.
  3. Click the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
Note: This action is irreversible and will remove all data associated with the account.

If you encounter an error:
  1. Check the error message displayed on the screen.
  2. Ensure your Instagram credentials are correct.
  3. Make sure your account is not temporarily blocked by Instagram.
  4. If the issue persists, try resetting the account or deleting and re-adding it.
  5. Contact support if the problem continues.
Note: Some errors may be caused by Instagram's rate limits or security measures.
