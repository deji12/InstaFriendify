Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about InstaFriendify? We’ve got answers! Here are some of the most common questions about our Instagram automation tool.

What is InstaFriendify?

InstaFriendify is an Instagram automation tool designed to help you manage your Close Friends list effortlessly. It allows you to add followers to your Close Friends list, scrape followers, and automate repetitive tasks.

How do I add followers to my Close Friends list?

Simply log in to your account, select the followers you want to add, and click the "Add to Close Friends" button. InstaFriendify will handle the rest automatically.

Can I manage multiple Instagram accounts?

Yes! InstaFriendify supports multiple Instagram accounts. You can easily switch between accounts and manage them all from one dashboard.

Is InstaFriendify safe to use?

Absolutely. We prioritize your account security and use advanced encryption to protect your data. Our tool is designed to comply with Instagram's guidelines to minimize risks.
